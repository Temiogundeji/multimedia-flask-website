welcome to our church
It's the start of an amazing journey of faith

The Foursquare Church, officially named the International Church of the Foursquare Gospel, is a Pentecostal denomination that resulted from the dynamic evangelistic ministry of Aimee Semple McPherson, who opened the historic Angelus Temple on Jan. 1, 1923. As time passed, Foursquare branched out to the rest of the World; we now have churches in most of the nations, including Hawaii,Alaska and Nigeria. The Foursquare found expression in Nigeria, which brought forth the Foursquare Church Ilaro, Ogun State, which Pastor (Dr.) Jesushina Coordinates and other co-ministers. The Bible says in Matthew 18:20; For where two or three are gathered together in my name, there am I in the midst of them. At every convention, we enjoy abundant fellowship with our brethren from all over the world. God’s presence is always in our midst. Do all you can to attend this meeting. Our Annual convention is a time to meet and fellowship with brethren from other parts of the country. We really enjoy the music and festive atmosphere in God’s presence. It is always a time of joy, dancing and love.

our belief
Our belief is based on core biblical truths we call the 22 tenets of faith. We believe in The Holy Scripture, The Plan of Redemption, The Baptism of the Holy Spirit, Moderation, Divine Healing, Heaven, Civil Government and lots of other amazing truths.
our faith
We believe that the Bible is God-inspired; perfect, with no mistakes; that God told certain men thousands of years ago what to write in it; that it is there to help us and to show everybody Jesus-the Son of God, the Savior of mankind; that it tells people how to live and be saved. (Matt. 24:35;2 Timothy 3:16,17; Psa. 119:105; 2Pet. 1:19-21; 2Tim. 2:15).
